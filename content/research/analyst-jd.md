# Hotel Markets Research Analyst — Job Description

**Title**: Hotel Markets Research Analyst (Contract / Internship)
**Reports to**: Nate Solomon, Hospitality Associate
**Employer of record**: Matthews Hotel Markets / Matthews Real Estate Investment Services
**Location**: Austin, TX (hybrid possible)
**Engagement type**: Independent contractor (1099) OR paid internship via St. Edward's / UT McCombs / UH Bauer academic-credit program
**Duration**: 26 weeks initial (covers two MHI cycles + first MHIO field period)
**Hours**: 20–30 hours/week, flexible. Spike to 40 hours/week during MHI release weeks (weeks 7-8, 19-20, 31-32, 43-44 in a 12-month cycle).
**Compensation**: $25–$35/hour (W-9 contract) OR academic credit + $500/month stipend (intern path).

---

## Responsibilities

- Build and maintain **Matthews Hotel Index (MHI)** quarterly cap-rate + price-per-key + RevPAR dataset across top 14-25 hotel MSAs. Source: public-data only (REIT 10-Qs, STR press releases, county appraisal districts, Trepp public summaries, CBRE / HVS free reports). See `mhi-public-data-source-map.md` for full source list.
- Build and maintain **Matthews Hotel Investor Outlook (MHIO)** annual survey: instrument design (existing draft in `investor-outlook-survey-instrument.md`), distribution coordination, response cleaning, cross-tab analysis, report drafting.
- **Research support for /insights pillar pages** (Hotel Cap Rates 2026, Hotel Valuation Guide, How to Sell a Hotel) — fact-checking, citation sourcing, chart creation.
- **Per-market page data** (14 metros across /markets/[city]) — local comp aggregation, cap rate trend commentary draft, current-listing roll-up.
- **Closed-deal page data** (rolling — every closed transaction documented at /closed/[slug]).
- **Trade-press pitch support**: pre-pitch fact-pack assembly for the PR consultant per Pitch Calendar Q2 2026.

## Skills required

- Excel / Google Sheets (advanced — pivot tables, lookups, regression, basic VBA helpful)
- SQL basic (for parsing REIT 10-Q tables; not strictly required if Excel-strong)
- Financial modeling fundamentals (cap rate math, debt service coverage, IRR)
- Crisp written English — will draft commentary that ships under broker bylines after editing
- PowerPoint or Figma for chart-making
- Public-records research (SEC EDGAR, county appraisal sites, government data portals)

## Skills nice-to-have

- Python for web scraping (REIT filings, county data, Trepp summaries)
- Tableau / Looker / Power BI
- Prior CRE / hospitality industry exposure
- Statistical analysis (regression, basic econometrics) for cross-tabbed survey analysis

## Interview structure (45-min Zoom)

1. **Background** (10 min) — walk through resume; what's drawn them to CRE / hospitality
2. **Excel test** (15 min) — sent ahead of interview: pull RevPAR data from Apple Hospitality's most recent 10-Q, weighted-average across their disclosed brand mix, compare to the prior quarter. Discuss approach.
3. **Writing sample** (10 min) — review their writing sample; ask them to summarize one 10-Q paragraph in 50 words
4. **Fit + culture** (10 min) — what they want out of the role; availability; commitment

- **Day 1**: Read `mhi-public-data-source-map.md`, `investor-outlook-survey-instrument.md`, and the v2 plan. Set up SEC EDGAR alerts on the 11 named REITs. Set up STR press release email subscription.
- **Day 2-3**: Build a starter spreadsheet pulling Apple Hospitality + Summit + Chatham Q1 2026 RevPAR by region. Cross-check against STR Top 25 data.
- **Day 4-5**: Draft the data layout for MHI v1's Austin metro page. Review with Nate.
- **Week 2**: Begin first MHI metro builds (Austin, Dallas, Houston).
- **Week 4**: First MHI v1 release-ready draft for Nate + Luke review.
