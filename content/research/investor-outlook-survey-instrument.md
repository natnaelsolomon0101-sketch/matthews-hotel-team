## Analysis framework

### Cross-tabs to run

1. AUM tier × hotel allocation (where is concentration)
2. Investor type × 2027 deployment intent (who's deploying)
3. Investor type × segment preference (where capital is rotating)
4. Geography preference × investor type (who's bidding which markets)
5. Cap rate expectation distribution by segment (median + IQR)
6. Top 3 concerns × investor type (PE concerns vs family office concerns)
7. Distress opportunity × deployment intent (correlation)

### Key derived metrics for headlines

- **% of investors planning to increase 2027 deployment** (headline metric — direct comparison to CBRE Investor Intentions)
- **Median Q4 2027 cap rate expectation, Sun Belt secondary select-service** (headline data point)
- **Top 3 segments by aggregate rank** (where capital wants to go)
- **Top 3 concerns by aggregate rank** (what's keeping investors up)
