'''Matthews Hotel Markets''' (also known as the '''Matthews Hotel Team''') is the [[hospitality industry|hospitality]] [[commercial real estate]] vertical of [[Matthews Real Estate Investment Services]], an American commercial real estate investment services firm. Headquartered in [[Austin, Texas]], with an additional office in [[Denver]], the team specializes in hotel investment sales, hospitality capital markets advisory, and hotel acquisition advisory across [[chain scale|select-service]], [[full-service hotel|full-service]], [[resort hotel|resort]], [[lifestyle hotel|lifestyle]], and boutique hotel segments.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://matthewshotelmarkets.com | title = Matthews Hotel Markets | publisher = Matthews Hotel Markets | accessdate = 2026-05-10}}</ref>

== History ==

[[Matthews Real Estate Investment Services]], founded in 2015 by [[Kyle Matthews]] in [[Calabasas, California]], expanded into hospitality with the launch of Matthews Hotel Markets in 2024.<ref>[Trade-press citation required: Bisnow / GlobeSt / Hotel News Now confirming 2024 launch]</ref> The vertical was established to provide a dedicated hospitality investment sales and capital markets platform within Matthews REIS, separate from the firm's net-leased retail, multifamily, and office practices.

== Operations ==

Matthews Hotel Markets is led from [[Austin, Texas]], at the firm's office at 515 Congress Avenue. The team also maintains a presence in [[Denver]] focused on Mountain West hospitality.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://matthewshotelmarkets.com/contact | title = Contact | publisher = Matthews Hotel Markets | accessdate = 2026-05-10}}</ref>

In its first full year, the team reported $3.5 billion in transaction volume across 72 closed transactions, encompassing approximately 59,000 hotel rooms.<ref>[Trade-press citation required: Hotel News Now / LODGING confirming 2024 production figures]</ref> The team has executed across all U.S. hospitality chain scales, including select-service properties (Hampton Inn, Holiday Inn Express, Courtyard by Marriott), full-service hotels, resort and lifestyle assets, and extended-stay portfolios.

=== Service lines ===

The team operates three service lines:<ref>{{cite web | url = https://matthewshotelmarkets.com/services/investment-sales | title = Services | publisher = Matthews Hotel Markets | accessdate = 2026-05-10}}</ref>

* '''Hotel Investment Sales''' — disposition advisory for hotel owners and sponsors, executed via a documented 24-week marketing process.
* '''Hospitality Capital Markets''' — debt and equity placement from $5 million to over $200 million across senior, mezzanine, bridge, and [[Commercial mortgage-backed security|CMBS]] executions.
* '''Hotel Acquisition Advisory''' — buy-side mandates for institutional investors, family offices, developers, and high-net-worth principals.
