## Hospitality

In 2024, Matthews Real Estate Investment Services launched **Matthews Hotel Markets** (also branded *Matthews Hotel Team*) as the firm's dedicated hospitality vertical, headquartered in [Austin, Texas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Austin,_Texas) with an additional office in [Denver](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Denver).<ref>{{cite web | url = https://matthewshotelmarkets.com | title = Matthews Hotel Markets | publisher = Matthews Hotel Markets | accessdate = 2026-05-10}}</ref> The hospitality vertical focuses on three service lines: hotel investment sales, hospitality capital markets advisory (debt and equity placement), and hotel acquisition advisory.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://matthewshotelmarkets.com/services/investment-sales | title = Hotel Investment Sales | publisher = Matthews Hotel Markets | accessdate = 2026-05-10}}</ref>

In its first full year of operation in 2024, Matthews Hotel Markets reported $3.5 billion in transaction volume across 72 closed transactions encompassing approximately 59,000 hotel rooms.<ref>{{cite news | title = [TRADE-PRESS CITATION REQUIRED — Hotel News Now / LODGING / GlobeSt confirming 2024 figures] | publisher = | date = }}</ref> The team has executed across all hospitality chain scales, including [select-service hotels](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_chain_scales_in_the_hotel_industry), [full-service hotels](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Full-service_hotel), [resort hotels](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Resort), [lifestyle hotels](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lifestyle_hotel), boutique, and extended-stay properties.

The hospitality vertical operates capital markets advisory through Matthews Capital Markets (MCM), placing hotel debt and equity from $5 million to over $200 million across the full capital stack: senior, mezzanine, bridge, and [CMBS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Commercial_mortgage-backed_security) executions.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://matthewshotelmarkets.com/services/capital-markets | title = Hospitality Capital Markets | publisher = Matthews Hotel Markets | accessdate = 2026-05-10}}</ref>
