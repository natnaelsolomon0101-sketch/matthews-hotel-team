## Outreach hygiene

- **One pitch per reporter at a time.** Don't pitch 3 reporters at the same outlet simultaneously — kills credibility.
- **Exclusive language is real.** "Exclusive" must mean exclusive — for the lead paragraph at minimum, ideally for first-publication.
- **Lead times**: trade press 1-2 weeks ahead of publication. Tier 1 typically 2-4 weeks.
- **Bcc no one.** Use FollowUpThen or a CRM to track responses, not bcc to inbox.
- **Reply within 4 hours** if a reporter responds. Trade-press cycles move fast.
- **Track in a placements log**: outlet, reporter, pitch date, response, placement date, placement URL, backlink confirmed, AI citation observed.
